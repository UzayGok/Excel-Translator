Needs python, openpyxl, selenium
Needs a chrome driver address in the same directory (settings.txt, first line)

The excel file must be in the same folder and the script.

Firstly, enter the file's name.
Secondly, enter the column(s) you wish to translate
Finally, enter the lines you wish to translate.

Do not interrupt the browser window. At best, do not even open it.

having wasted your time.
